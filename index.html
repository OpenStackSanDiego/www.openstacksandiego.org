Getting To Know Us

Every 3rd Wednesday

We meet once a month to share our knowledge and experiences with the growing and changing DevOps discipline. Typically our meetings last 1-2 hours, and are centered around talks that show off new tools and challenging experiences. Topics generally flow into open discussion at the end.

Whether building a web service, mobile app, or gaming backend, we are passionate about the development tools and organizational practices that will make it work.

Who is DevOps?

DevOps is a software development method that emphasizes communication, collaboration, integration, automation, and measurement of cooperation between software developers and other IT professionals.

It is a growing discipline that sits at the crossroad of Development, QA, and Delivery. Wide ranging technical and social strategies are used to streamline and automate the work needed to get code from developers to the customer's hands.
