Getting To Know Us

Every 4th Wednesday

We meet once a month to share our knowledge and experiences around the projects that make up OpenStack. Our monthly meetups include talks by developers and operators of OpenStack alike as well as hands on events. We welcome OpenStack novices and experts to participate.

We maintain a welcoming and friendly group for everyone. Please read the OpenStack Code of Conduct.

OpenStack software controls large pools of compute, storage, and networking resources throughout a datacenter, managed through a dashboard or via the OpenStack API. OpenStack works with popular enterprise and open source technologies making it ideal for heterogeneous infrastructure.
